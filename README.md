## What is Robotics Engineering?

* I believe Robotics Engineering is the intersection of the best parts of Electrical, Mechanical, and Computer science. By combining theory with design, application, process integration, and embedded systems, I can design autonomous systems from the ground up. I have all the skills to develop computer vision, microprocessors, and robot arms alike, as well as integrate them all cohesively.
* It's a little bit of everything, and that versatility is what's so exciting about automation and robotics.
